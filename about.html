I started in SEO in 2018 by chance — a local business owner asked me to help them show up on Google Maps. Three weeks later, they were ranking. I was hooked. Not by rankings, but by the direct connection between what I fixed technically and what happened to real businesses.

That agency experience taught me something most freelancers don’t have — how to run Local SEO campaigns at scale, across dozens of clients simultaneously, with the rigour that only comes from accountability to agency clients paying thousands per month.

That transformation — from invisible to dominant — is what I build.

How I work

Three principles every project runs on

Vertical depth over breadth

I work in 7 industries, not 70. HVAC, Plumbing, Roofing, Dental, Restoration, Pest Control, and Travel have different ranking patterns, review dynamics, and citation ecosystems. Knowing each cold is the difference between average and excellent results.

Calls, not rankings

Map Pack #1 means nothing if the phone isn't ringing. I track GBP calls, direction requests, and lead volume — and attribute revenue impact wherever possible. Rankings are a means to an end.

Agency rigour, freelancer care

Agency experience gave me the systems and methodology. Working directly with you means you get the full attention — not a junior account manager. Every deliverable is done by me, not delegated.
